## ⚠️ The Problem: AI is Reactive
Right now, most AI assistants only work when you ask them to. You type a prompt, and they give an answer. When you close the tab, they go to sleep. 

But humans don't work like that. We need a system that thinks even when we are not typing.

## ✨ The Solution: Proactive AI
**LIGHT LPCA** is designed to be proactive. It is a continuous AI that runs in the background. It doesn't just wait for your questions; it actively reads, thinks, and prepares things for you while you are away. 

---

## 🌍 How LIGHT Can Help You
LIGHT analyzes your ongoing work and autonomously decides how it can assist you. While it doesn't guarantee a specific outcome every time, it can surprise you by proactively doing things like:

- **👨‍💻 For Programmers & Developers:** It might read your recent code, notice a logical mistake, suggest a new feature, or help complete an unfinished script.
- **✍️ For Writers:** It could review your draft overnight and suggest a better ending, or point out a missing piece in your story.
- **🎓 For Students & Teachers:** It might organize your messy study notes, create a summary of your recent research, or prepare a quick structure for your next class.
- **👔 For Non-Tech Users:** It can automatically track your unfinished daily tasks, organize your scattered thoughts, and help you pick up exactly where you left off yesterday.
